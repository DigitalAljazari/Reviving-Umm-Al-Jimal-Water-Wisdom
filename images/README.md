# Project Images

This folder contains selected field documentation and visual materials related to Umm Al-Jimal, the proposed Deir Al-Kahf pilot site, and the development of **Reviving Umm Al-Jimal's Water Wisdom**.

The photographs support research, education, project documentation, and responsible public communication.

## Image Context

The images document:

- Field research at Umm Al-Jimal.
- Preliminary assessment of the proposed Deir Al-Kahf pilot landscape.
- Early field measurement.
- The agricultural and environmental context of Jordan's Northern Badia.

The photographs represent preliminary field documentation. They do not demonstrate completed construction, final technical approval, or verified pilot performance.
