# Reviving Umm Al-Jimal Water Wisdom

## About the Project

Reviving Umm Al-Jimal Water Wisdom is a research and innovation initiative by Digital Al-Jazari for AI & Research Innovation. The project studies the ancient rainwater harvesting systems of Umm Al-Jimal in Jordan and combines them with artificial intelligence and digital simulation to develop sustainable solutions for water scarcity, climate resilience, ecosystem restoration, and peacebuilding in arid regions.

## Objectives

- Preserve the water heritage of Umm Al-Jimal.
- Develop AI-assisted digital models of ancient water systems.
- Support climate adaptation in drylands.
- Restore native vegetation.
- Promote sustainable water management.
- Share open scientific knowledge with researchers worldwide.
